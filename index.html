What are Keyboards?

Keyboards are a necessity in the digital age, as conveying thoughts and ideas to others through computers is an integral part of modern life. But as much as we use them, keyboards often get overlooked. So what's going on there? And why do some people spend way too much money to create their own? This page tries to answer these questions along with tips and info on how to build your own.

Switches

Switches are what physically registers a keypress, and are a critical part to the sound of the keyboard. Learn more about switches here.

Boards + Mounting Styles

The size of your board obviously is a big part of the keyboard, and deciding the right one is important in the process. Mounting styles are how the plate/pcb are set inside the board, affecting sound and feel. Learn more about boards and mounting styles here.

Keycaps

Keycaps are what covers the switches and what your fingers press. They can affect feel, sound, and of course looks. Learn more about keycaps here.
